Fight Club

This project entailed the choreography, rehersal, recording, retargeting, & post-processing of motion capture data. All assests came from Unreal Market place, predecesor to Fab, sounds came from freesounds.org, & all lighting, layout, and rendering work was done in Unreal Engine 5. For this project, the data was brought into Motion Builder for retargetting & cleanup, before being exported and brought into Unreal for lighting, camera blocking, & rendering.
