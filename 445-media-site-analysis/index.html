How would I revise my media categorization?

The revisions I would add would be a break down each of my categories into the platforms they are consumed on. For example video games would be broken down into PS5, PC, Mobile, etc, and music could be split into Sotify, Vinyl, Live, etc.

What would I add to my heirarchy of media?

I would add the inclusion of social media as a new category of media consumption. On top of that I would also look into adding a new method of organizing my heirarchy into a tree design similar to the ones used in family trees. This might provide a simplar way of visualizing and understanding the relation between all the different types of media.
